Resistors in Series & Parallel

Build circuits to understand how total resistance changes when resistors are in series vs. parallel.

Series Circuit

In a series circuit, resistors are connected end-to-end. The total resistance is the sum of all individual resistances.

R_eq = R₁ + R₂ + R₃

Parallel Circuit

In a parallel circuit, resistors are connected on separate branches. The total resistance is less than any of the individual resistances.

1/R_eq = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + 1/R₃
