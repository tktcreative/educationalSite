Ray Diagrams: Convex Mirror

Place an object at different positions to see how a convex mirror always forms a virtual, erect, and diminished image.

Ray Diagram Rules

  • A ray parallel to the principal axis reflects as if it came from the Focal Point (F).
  • A ray directed towards the Focal Point (F) reflects parallel to the principal axis.
  • A ray directed towards the Center of Curvature (C) reflects back along the same path.

How to Use:

Click and drag the candle (the object) along the principal axis to change its position and observe the resulting image.
