The Reactivity Series

Drop different metals into salt solutions to see which ones are reactive enough to displace others.

What is the Reactivity Series?

The reactivity series is a list of metals arranged in order of their decreasing reactivity. A more reactive metal will displace a less reactive metal from its salt solution.

How to Use:

  • Drag a piece of metal from the top bar.
  • Drop it into one of the beakers containing a salt solution.
  • Observe if a reaction takes place. If the metal is more reactive than the metal in the solution, it will displace it!
  • Click Reset to start the experiment over.
