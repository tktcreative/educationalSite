Types of Chemical Reactions

Combine reactants to see examples of Combination, Decomposition, and Displacement reactions.

Reaction Types

Combination Reaction

Hydrogen and Oxygen combine to form a single product, Water.

Decomposition Reaction

Hydrogen Peroxide breaks down into two simpler substances, Water and Oxygen.

Displacement Reaction

The more reactive Zinc displaces the less reactive Copper from Copper Sulfate solution.
